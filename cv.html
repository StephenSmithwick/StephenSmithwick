Profile

Steve is a technical director who quickly assimilates new technologies and information. He is a strong leader who delivers strategic business results.

Steve is an advocate of Agile development because of its collaborative approach to solving problems. By working closely with users to define a set of requirements which are then continuously tested and released on a fortnightly basis, it is possible to ensure the users requirements are showcased early and often. This no surprises approach ensures the best possible outcome for all stakeholders.

His enthusiasm for Agile development is contagious. He introduced and developed an agile workflow at RateCity. RateCity uses Agile techniques to create, prioritise, track and follow user stories and projects. Daily stand-ups provide an opportunity for the team to collaborate and share with the team about their current progress.

Steve is a passionate Test Driven and Behaviour Driven Developer (TDD/BDD). He believes that by defining functional requirements and translating these into continuous and automated tests it is possible to mitigate risks, ensure that bugs are identified before release and that the product always meets the specified requirements.

He has excellent organisational, interpersonal and business communication skills with the flexibility required to adjust to rapidly changing schedules and shifting priorities.
